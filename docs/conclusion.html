Conclusion

Summary of Findings

The Visual Question Answering (VQA) model demonstrates a robust capability to interpret and respond to visual and textual queries. Through extensive training and evaluation across multiple noise levels, the model maintained impressive accuracy and showcased resilience.

Future Work

There are several avenues for further research and development:

  • Enhancing the model to support additional languages and improve multilingual performance.
  • Exploring more sophisticated noise handling techniques to further enhance robustness.
  • Integrating the model into real-world applications and fine-tuning it for specific use cases.
